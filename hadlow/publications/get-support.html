    Introduction

    The coronavirus (COVID-19) restrictions are hard on everyone. Whilst some continue on unaffected, many people are struggling to carry on during this hard time.
    Loneliness, depression and anxiety can affect everyone, even if you've never experienced it before.
    We are humans too, and we want to be here to give you support to help you move through this hard time.

    How we can help

    Virtual Sunday sessions

    As promised, we will try to be open every week on Zoom, whilst we may not be able to chat for hours, sometimes the sense of togetherness can bring ease to people and make them feel calm.
    It may be a first step as you are able to meet and get comfortable with us.
    If you have any questions about these sessions, you can ask on Facebook or drop us an email anytime.

    Have a chat

    Many of our friendly community are more than willing to chat with you. Whether you want to scream, shout, rant, cry or just talk, we will be here with an ear to listen and a (virtual) shoulder to cry on.

    Email us

    Getting your feelings written out is sometimes easier than talking them out. If you wanna talk out your feelings or just want a 'pen pal'. Drop our dedicated email a message and someone will reply and be a friend.

    TSC - Here to help
    Email: here-to-help@tonbridgespiritualist.com

    If you want to talk to a professional, please send your email to Samaritans.

    If you need further support

    Whilst we want to help you, sometimes you need more support.
    You can talk to Samaritans 24/7.

    Samaritans
    Email: jo@samaritans.org
    Phone: 116 123

    The charity Mind has given ideas of where to start to get support if you are struggling.
