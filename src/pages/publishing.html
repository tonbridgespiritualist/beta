    Once you have made your service, there are three steps you need to do to publish it to tonbridgespiritualist.com.

    1. Get a service.tonbridgespiritualist.com domain from the Digital Service.
    2. Upload your NDK to a private repository within the tonbridgespiritualist GitHub organisation.

      Don't forget to remove the node_modules folder before though.

    3. Upload your dist folder to the public repository created by the Digital Service, linked to your service domain.
    If you have a service start page, you should open a pull request to integrate this into tonbridgespiritualist.com in the main directory. It should share it's filename with your service domain.

    For example if your service is hosted on watch-past-session[...], your start page should be called watch-past-session.html.

    If you need help, you can contact the Development Support Office.
